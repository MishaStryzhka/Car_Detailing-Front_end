About Us

VIP Car Detailing is a professional car detailing service in Prague, working with luxury and everyday cars. Our prices are affordable, and the quality of our services is appreciated by hundreds of satisfied customers. We offer a wide range of services, from standard car washes to full detailing. Trust your car to us – we'll make it clean, shiny, and as good as new!
