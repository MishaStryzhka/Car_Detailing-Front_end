Ceramic Coating

Ceramic coating for your car is the most reliable protection against all negative influences!

We use premium ceramic coatings from renowned manufacturers, providing long-lasting protection for the paintwork from UV rays, water, dirt stains, acid rain, scratches, and other external impacts.

Our experienced staff carefully apply the ceramic coating according to all technological standards, ensuring even coverage of the body and a long-lasting effect.

Trust your car to the professionals at VIP Car Detailing – we’ll make it shiny and protected!

Additional Benefits of Ceramic Coating:

  • Unmatched Shine

    Your car will shine with a deep, mirror-like gloss.

  • Excellent Hydrophobic Effect

    Water will easily roll off the surface, leaving no streaks.

  • Protection from UV Rays

    Ceramic coating protects the paint from fading and dulling.

  • Protection from Chemicals

    Ceramic coating creates a strong barrier that protects the paint from acid rain and other chemicals.

  • Increased Scratch Resistance

    Ceramic coating increases the hardness of the paint, reducing the risk of new scratches.

  • Long-lasting Effect

    Ceramic coating will maintain its protective effect for several years.
