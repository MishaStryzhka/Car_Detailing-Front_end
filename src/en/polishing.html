Car Polishing

Car polishing is not just about shine, but about restoring and protecting the paintwork!

We use professional polishing machines and pastes of various abrasiveness to remove fine scratches, bird marks, sap, and other paint imperfections.

After polishing, your car will shine with a deep, mirror-like gloss, and its paint will be protected from external elements.
